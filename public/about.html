About Me

I’ve always been a storyteller at heart. I grew up in Ardmore, Oklahoma, dreaming up characters and worlds that felt bigger than my small-town life. After earning a degree in Creative Writing and a minor in Psychology, I moved to Oklahoma City, where I balance life as a Product Manager with my love for writing stories that feel true to who I am.

I write about people who are flawed, hopeful, and real—characters who reflect the communities and cultures I come from. My biggest goal is to make readers feel seen and to use my stories to inspire empathy and change.

When I’m not writing, I’m either reading, plotting my next project, or losing hours to The Sims.
